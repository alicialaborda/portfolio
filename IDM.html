IDM

Case

IDM wants to implement Salesforce Marketing Cloud into their Business. They need a Doble Optin process for the subscription to the Newsletters, html templates for the email communications and an unsubscribe process for clients who desire to opt-out. who purchase outside the ordinary registration process from the website and points of sale. The purpose is to be able to incorporate the contact data of these clients into the Salesforce universe. The Marketing platform will be used to incorporate the contact data of the clients and to send these clients Email communications. Additionally, they need a solution to be able to track the email performance.

Solution

The Doble-Optin and the Unsubscribe Landing Pages implemented in Marketing Cloud will be prepared for multilanguage (German, English and Italian). All processing of what to display and the update of attributes (consent/s and date/s) in the CRM will be developed using AMPScript code.
